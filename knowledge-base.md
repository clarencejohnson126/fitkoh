## CLASSES OFFERED

All classes are beginner-friendly unless specified otherwise. Classes are booked via the **Rezerv app**. Arrive 10 minutes before class and scan the QR code at the gym entrance.

### Fitness Classes
- **HYROX** -- Competition-style functional fitness training
- **TRX** -- Suspension training for functional movement, strength, lean muscle
- **Metcon** -- Metabolic conditioning; structured work/rest for maximum efficiency. Focus on form over max weight. Most guests' favorite class.
- **Kettlebells** -- Hip-driven movement patterns, proper lifting technique, fat loss, strength, lean muscle
- **HIIT** -- 45-minute high-intensity interval training using barbell, kettlebell, TRX, and bodyweight exercises. Suitable beginners to advanced.
- **Glutes & Abs** -- Targeted lower body and core work (Premium packages only)
- **Mobility** -- Flexibility and movement quality (Premium packages only)
- **Beach Training** -- Every Saturday, the whole gym trains at Ban Tai Beach to finish off the week

### Martial Arts Classes
- **Muay Thai** -- Uses 8 points of contact (punches, kicks, elbows, knee strikes). Suitable for complete beginners to pro fighters. Up to 12 classes per week.
- **Boxing Drills** -- Boxing-specific technique and conditioning
- **BJJ (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu)** -- Ground-based martial art

### Wellness Classes
- **Yoga** -- Daily morning practice led by experienced instructors. Focus on breath awareness, patience, and gratitude. 3x/week in most packages.
- **Breathwork & Ice Baths** -- Recovery and mental resilience sessions (Premium only, or pay per session)
- **Jungle Run** -- Scenic mountain run through Maenam jungle. Option 1: 4km to viewpoint. Option 2: 6km (advanced) to Khun Si viewpoint and waterfall. Transport back to FitKoh provided.

## FAQ -- HEALTH & SAFETY

**What if I feel unwell?**
Notify staff and refrain from training until recovered.

**Hydration:** Drink 3-4 liters of water per day. Free refillable water available. Bottled water not sold.

**Animals:** Avoid engaging with street dogs. Do not encourage animals onto FitKoh property.

**Vaccinations:** Not mandatory for Thailand entry. Recommended: Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, Tetanus. Consult healthcare provider.

**Travel insurance:** Highly recommended. Physical exercise involves risk of injury.

**Visa:** Most visitors receive 30 or 60 day visa exemption on arrival. Check with Thai embassy. Visa extensions possible at local immigration office before expiry.
